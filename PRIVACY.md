Privacy Policy for 飞书文档导出器                                             
                       
  Last updated: 2026-03-23                                                      
  
  This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.     
                       
  All operations are performed entirely on the user's local device:             
  - Document content is processed locally and never sent to any server
  - Exported files are saved directly to the user's local disk                  
  - No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
                                                                                
  If you have any questions, please open an issue on GitHub.
